# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Customers**: Moviegoers who want to search movies, check reviews/showtimes, and book tickets easily using either a visual UI or the natural language AI assistant.
- **Hall Managers**: Staff managing screening schedules, screen configurations, and show properties for their assigned screens under strict scheduling rules.
- **Admins**: Power users overseeing the system catalog, user roles/permissions, scheduling overrides, and auditing via activity logs.

## Product Purpose
CineBook is an AI-powered movie booking platform. It aims to make movie ticket reservation friction-free through two primary paths: a classic intuitive mobile/web flow and a highly capable natural language chatbot. The system enforces strict scheduling integrity for managers while ensuring a consistent, real-time concurrency-safe booking experience for customers.

## Brand Personality
- **Cinematic & Immersive**: Emphasizes media content (poster art, trailer previews) in a premium dark environment.
- **Community & Letterboxd-Inspired**: Clean, content-first typography with rating systems and visual grids rather than complex corporate structures.
- **Focused & Professional**: Prioritizes clear, structured presentation; rejects distracting animations, popups, and visual noise.

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS Clichés**: Bland warm-white layouts with standard gray outline cards and tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows.
- **Ad-Heavy Ticketing Portals**: High-density pages crammed with banner ads, popups, and aggressive upselling.
- **Over-designed AI/Crypto Slop**: Superfluous gradients, default heavy glassmorphism, glowing text, and unnecessary bouncing animations.

## Design Principles
1. **Content First**: Treat movie posters, titles, and show details as the core visual anchors.
2. **Clear Rhythm & Focus**: Use spacing and high-contrast typography hierarchy to direct attention to active selections (seats, showtimes).
3. **Restrained Elegance**: Use solid colors, structured lines, and micro-interactions rather than decorative styling to establish premium feel.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Basic Standard**: Adhere to browser/OS defaults for text scaling and accessibility settings, maintaining high contrast (≥4.5:1) for body copy.
